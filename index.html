A Taste of Tradition & Luxury in Every Bite

Welcome to Cherry Garden Bakery & Cafe, Titabor's most beloved destination for exquisite baked goods and delightful cafe experiences. Located in the heart of Jorhat, Assam, we have been serving our community with passion and perfection.

From custom birthday cakes that make celebrations memorable to artisan pastries baked fresh daily, every item at Cherry Garden is crafted with love using the finest ingredients. Our cozy cafe atmosphere provides the perfect setting to enjoy our gourmet pizzas, traditional sweets, and premium coffee selections.
